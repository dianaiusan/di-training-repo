# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) 

## Do I have to pay money to attend your courses and training events?

NAISS training courses are free of charge to all users and prospective users of the infrastructure.  NAISS training is an integral part of the service.  The training is  essential for the infrastructure to be useful to Swedish research.

## Who can participate in NAISS training?

All users and prospective users of the infrastructure are invited to attend our events.
