# NAISS Training

These pages guide you through the NAISS training offer. Here you can find courses and materials designed to help you use NAISS services effectively.

NAISS training is an integral part of the [National Academic Infrastructure for Supercomputing in Sweden (NAISS)](https://www.naiss.se/). Training is essential to make NAISS services accessible to Swedish academic researchers. We provide a substantial course portfolio, together with online materials suitable for self-study.
